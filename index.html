Eligibility Criteria

You must

Live in the UK, Channel Islands or Isle of Man and have lived there for at least 6 of the last 12 months

Be registered with a General Practitioner in that location

Start and end your trip in the UK

Be in the UK when you buy your policy

When cover doesn't apply:

If you travel against the latest FCDO travel advice

If buying insurance with the intention of making a claim

If you are already aware of something that's likely to lead to a claim

If you are travelling against medical advice
